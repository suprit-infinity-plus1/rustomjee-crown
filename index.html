Frequently Asked Question

Rustomjee Crown at Prabhadevi isnt just a residence; its a landmark of luxury. Experience expansive living spaces, serene surroundings, and world-class amenitiescrafted to bring you comfort, elegance, and the lifestyle you truly deserve.

The Rustomjee Crown project is located in Prabhadevi, South Mumbai. For more information, you can check out the location on Google Maps.

The configurations available at Rustomjee Crown are 3 BHK Residences (1335-1483 sq. feet), 4 BHK Residences (1694-2599 sq. feet) and 5 BHK Residences (3019-3088 sq. feet).

There are more than 66 modern amenities at Rustomjee Crown, including a Viewing Deck, Aroma Garden, Pool Amphitheatre, Hot Tubs, Solitude Forest, Lagoon Pool, Sunken Court, Cloudwalk, and English Court.

To enquire about unit pricing and availability, click on the Enquire Now button provided on the website and fill out the necessary details.

The MahaRERA registration number for Rustomjee Crown Phase 1 - Tower A and B is P51900003268, and Phase 2 - Tower C is P51900006367.
